Autonomy with accountability

Powerful, not reckless.

An agent that can touch your filesystem must earn your trust. Every layer is designed for it.

◈ Clearance gates

Every risky tool call — writes, shell, deletes — pauses for your approval. Allow once, always, or deny. Full audit trail, scope-limited paths.

◈ Plan mode & rollback

Flip to Plan mode and the agent researches and proposes without writing a byte. Checkpoints on every turn — one /undo rolls it all back.

◈ Local by design

Sessions, memories, and secrets stay on your disk. AES-256-GCM encryption, OS-keychain token vault, and nothing phones home. Ever.
